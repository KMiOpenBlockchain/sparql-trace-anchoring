Cookies

This Open Blockchain Experimentation area (blockchain.kmi.open.ac.uk) and its sub-sites are static websites that do not use cookies.

If you have visited or are logged into the Open University's main website (open.ac.uk), before you visit this website, you may see additional cookies.
What additional cookies you see will depend on what you agreed to on the main Open University site, and whether you are logged in to the main Open Univerity website.
For full details of their cookie policy, please see: The Open University Cookie Policy


Links to other websites
